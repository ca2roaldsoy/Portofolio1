About Letha

At Letha we believe in creating quality, handcrafted leather products that benefit both our customers and the environment.
We use only organic leather, and ensure that from start to finish our products are made sustainably.

We have been in operation since 2009 when our founder Claude Harmison started Letha from his garage. It has since grown to a staff of 5 passionate people.

Currently we only offer two options for our leather shoes as we believe that you should focus your efforts and ‘do what you do well’.
We are looking to expand our range in the near future.
